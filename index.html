About Hasnain Imtiaz

I'm Hasnain Imtiaz, a trailblazer at the crossroads of machine learning, data science, and communication studies. CGPA 3.43 — Joint Merit Scholarship awarded by the University of Dhaka and the Government of Bangladesh.

Over the past five years, I have worked with researchers, healthcare professionals, NGOs, think tanks, and technology-focused teams to design studies, develop research methodologies, conduct statistical analyses, and translate complex data into practical insights.

I currently serve as a Communication Expert at PPRC, working on stakeholder and donor engagement, policy communication, and strategic outreach for projects funded by UNICEF, IDRC, and UHC initiatives, under the guidance of Dr. Hossain Zillur Rahman.
